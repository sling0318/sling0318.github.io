# 卫星边缘计算 [Zhang, Zhenjiang, Wenyu Zhang, and Fan-Hsun Tseng. "Satellite mobile edge computing: Improving QoS of high-speed satellite-terrestrial networks using edge computing techniques." IEEE network 33.1 (2019): 70-76.](https://sci-hub.mksa.top/10.1109/MNET.2018.1800172) 综述性文章，简述了目前边缘计算的问题。 [Xie, Renchao, et al. "Satellite-terrestrial integrated edge computing networks: architecture, challenges, and open issues." Ieee Network 34.3 (2020): 224-231.]() 综述性文章，提出了一种新的架构 [Kim, Taeyeoun, and Jihwan P. Choi. "Performance analysis of satellite server mobile edge computing architecture." 2020 IEEE 92nd Vehicular Technology Conference (VTC2020-Fall). IEEE, 2020.APA](https://sci-hub.mksa.top/https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/9348834/) 证明了在卫星上卸载计算的延迟比返回陆地云数据中心更低。 [Denby, Bradley, and Brandon Lucia. "Orbital edge computing: Nanosatellite constellations as a new class of computer system." Proceedings of the Twenty-Fifth International Conference on Architectural Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems. 2020.](https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/3373376.3378473) 在轨边缘计算，使用纳米卫星作为计算卫星，提出了一种新架构和仿真框架 [Wang, Yuxuan, et al. "A game-theoretic approach to computation offloading in satellite edge computing." IEEE Access 8 (2019): 12510-12520.](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/stamp/stamp.jsp?arnumber=8945402) 考虑了间歇性通信，使用博弈论寻找纳什均衡的方式寻找最佳卸载策略。 [Yan, Lei, et al. "SatEC: A 5G satellite edge computing framework based on microservice architecture." Sensors 19.4 (2019): 831.](https://www.mdpi.com/1424-8220/19/4/831/pdf) 提出了一种卫星边缘计算框架（基于java）。 # 推荐系统 [Wang, Pengfei, et al. "KERL: A knowledge-guided reinforcement learning model for sequential recommendation." Proceedings of the 43rd International ACM SIGIR conference on research and development in Information Retrieval. 2020.](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1An5YXDvQ1K0A1AwpJG-I4gTbIQ26gUDS/view)
